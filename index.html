The Story

Meet Aria Sharma

A Journey of Passion & Elegance

From a small studio in Jaipur to dressing celebrities and brides across the globe, Aria Couture represents the pinnacle of Indian craftsmanship meeting contemporary design.

2008

The Beginning

Fresh from studying fashion design at NIFT, Aria started with a small collection of 12 pieces, each hand-embroidered by local artisans in Jaipur.

2012

First Fashion Week

Debuted at Lakme Fashion Week with a collection inspired by Mughal architecture. The collection was an instant hit, attracting attention from Bollywood stylists.

2016

International Recognition

Featured in Vogue India and showcased at London Fashion Week. Expanded the atelier to include a team of 50 master craftsmen.

2020

Digital Revolution

Launched the online boutique, bringing the Aria Couture experience to discerning customers worldwide, while maintaining the essence of personalized service.

Today

A Legacy Continues

With over 5,000 happy brides and a celebrity clientele, Aria Couture continues to create timeless pieces that celebrate Indian craftsmanship.
